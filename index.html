About Us

At Dried Rite, we specialize in creating premium freeze-dried fruit and floral blends that infuse beautifully with hot water. Our flagship line, Steep and Sip, brings you healthful, aromatic, and flavorful brews straight from the heart of Kerala.

How to Use

1. Open the jar and add 1–2 teaspoons of your favorite blend into a cup.
2. Pour hot water (not boiling) over it.
3. Let steep for 5–7 minutes.
4. Stir and enjoy your all-natural fruit tea.
Optional: Add honey or lemon for taste.
