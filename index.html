How to Use

  1. Add 1–2 teaspoons of the blend into a cup.
  2. Pour hot (not boiling) water over it.
  3. Steep for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Stir and enjoy your natural fruit tea.
  5. Optional: Add honey or lemon for taste.
